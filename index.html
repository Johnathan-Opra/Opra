06 · FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

  • What services does Opra offer?
    Opra offers four core services: operational websites, business platforms and dashboards, workflow automation, and AI systems. Every service is designed to reduce manual work and give growing businesses full operational visibility.
  • How long does a project take?
    Most automation and platform projects deliver in 2 to 4 weeks. We scope, price, and plan the full build before writing a single line of code. Timelines and costs are fixed before we start.
  • Do you work with small businesses?
    Yes. Our target clients are service businesses, agencies, and operationally heavy SMBs with 1 to 50 people. If manual processes are visibly costing your team time and money, you are the right fit. We do not work with enterprises or large corporations.
  • Where does Opra operate?
    We are based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We work remotely with clients across South Africa and take on international projects. There are no geographic restrictions on what we can build or support.
  • What is a client portal and do I need one?
    A client portal gives your clients secure access to project status, documents, invoices, and communication in one place. No scattered email threads. If you manage multiple clients and spend time chasing updates or resending files, a portal typically pays for itself within weeks.
  • How much does business automation cost?
    Pricing depends on scope and complexity. We provide fixed quotes before any work begins. No surprises. Book a free 20-minute consultation and we will scope and price your specific requirements within 24 hours.
