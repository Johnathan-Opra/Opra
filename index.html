FAQ
How long does a project take?
Depends on scope. A simple website takes 1–2 weeks. A full operational system takes 4–8 weeks. We scope everything before we start so you always know exactly what to expect and when.
How much does it cost?
We use fixed project pricing scoped per engagement. Every project is quoted individually after a free consultation. There are no packages or published rates. You will always know the full price before any work begins.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. Every project can move into a monthly support retainer. Retainers cover maintenance, optimisation, monitoring, and ongoing improvements to everything we have built together. Retainer options are discussed at project handover.
Do I need a technical background to work with you?
No. We handle all the technical work. You tell us what your business needs and we build it. We document everything clearly so you understand what was built and how it works. You never need to touch a line of code.
