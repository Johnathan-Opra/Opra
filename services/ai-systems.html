05 · Questions

Common questions,
straight answers.

  • Is this just a chatbot?
    No. A chatbot answers questions. An AI system does work. We build AI into your actual operational workflows: generating proposals, processing documents, triaging emails, routing requests, and producing reports. It operates inside the tools your team already uses and produces outputs your team can act on.
  • What tools can AI be configured into?
    We connect AI to the tools your business already uses. Google Workspace, HubSpot, QuickBooks, Xero, Canva, DocuSign, Notion, Airtable, and more. The goal is to get AI working inside your existing setup, not to replace your tools with new ones.
  • What is AI Operations?
    AI Operations is Opra's core AI service. It comes in two tiers. The Starter tier connects AI to your existing tools and activates specific workflows: lead triage, invoice chasing, proposal generation, contract review, campaign creation, and more. The Custom tier builds a bespoke AI system designed around your specific business requirements.
  • Do I need technical knowledge to use AI Systems?
    No. Every AI system we build is configured to operate inside the tools your team already knows. The AI runs behind the scenes. Your team sees outputs in the tools they use every day. Full training and documentation are included in every project.
