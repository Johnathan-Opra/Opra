02 · What we automate

Six types of automation.

CRM
CRM integrations

New leads flow automatically into your CRM from every source. No manual entry, no missed leads, no data living in two places at once.

Onboarding
Client onboarding

When a client signs, the automation takes over. Welcome sequences, document collection, system setup, and kickoff scheduling handled without anyone touching it.

Lead routing
Lead capture and routing

Inbound leads captured from every channel and routed to the right person instantly. No leads sitting in an inbox waiting for someone to notice them.

Approvals
Approval workflows

Approval requests routed to the right person, chased automatically if they don't respond, and logged when they do. No bottlenecks from approvals stuck in email.

Reporting
Reporting automation

Weekly and monthly reports generated automatically from your data and sent to the right people. No one spends time pulling spreadsheets together.

Notifications
Notifications and task routing

The right people get notified at the right time. Tasks created, assigned, and tracked automatically so nothing gets missed because someone forgot to tell someone.

04 · Questions

Common questions,
straight answers.

  • What can business automation do?
    Automation handles the repetitive, rule-based work your team currently does manually. Common examples: new leads automatically entered into your CRM, clients automatically sent onboarding documents after signing, approval requests routed to the right person and chased if they don't respond, and weekly reports generated and sent without anyone touching a spreadsheet.
  • What tools do you connect?
    We connect the tools your business already uses. Google Workspace, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Airtable, Notion, Slack, WhatsApp Business, QuickBooks, Xero, DocuSign, and more. If a tool has an API or webhook, we can connect it. We scope the exact integrations during the consultation.
  • Will my team need technical knowledge to use the automation?
    No. Automation is built to run in the background without anyone having to understand how it works. Your team uses the same tools they already use. The automation runs invisibly behind them. Full documentation and training are included in every project.
  • What is client onboarding automation?
    Client onboarding automation replaces the manual process of sending welcome emails, collecting documents, scheduling kickoff calls, and setting up the client in your systems. When a new client signs, the automation handles every step automatically. No one needs to remember to do it, and nothing gets missed.
