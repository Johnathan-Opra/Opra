05 · Questions

Common questions,
straight answers.

  • What is a client portal?
    A client portal is a secure, logged-in space where your clients can track project progress, access documents, submit requests, and communicate with your team. It replaces the email threads, WhatsApp messages, and spreadsheet updates that slow everyone down.
  • What is an internal dashboard?
    An internal dashboard gives your team real-time visibility over operations. It pulls data from your existing tools and displays it in one place so you can make decisions without chasing spreadsheets or asking five people for updates.
  • Can a platform connect to our existing tools?
    Yes. Every platform we build is designed to connect to the tools your business already uses. CRMs, project management tools, Google Workspace, invoicing systems, and more. We scope the integrations during the consultation.
  • How is a platform different from a website?
    A website is public-facing and largely static. A platform is an application. It has user authentication, data that changes in real time, and logic that responds to what users do. It runs your operations rather than describing them.
