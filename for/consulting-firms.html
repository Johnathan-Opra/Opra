The problem

Your consultants are
billing hours. Admin
is consuming them.

Consulting firms sell expertise. But a significant share of consultant time goes to things that have nothing to do with expertise: proposal writing, status updates, report compilation, onboarding new clients. Systems fix this.

Proposals take too long to write. Each one starts from scratch or from a template that needs heavy editing. Senior time spent on formatting instead of thinking.

Client communication is scattered. Updates go over email. Deliverables shared via Drive links in chat. No single place where the client can see engagement status.

Onboarding new clients is inconsistent. Each consultant does it differently. Some things get missed. The experience depends on who picked up the phone.

Leadership has no cross-engagement visibility. Knowing the status of all active engagements means asking every consultant. There is no dashboard, no overview, no single source of truth.

What we build

Three systems built for consulting operations.

Client-facing
Client portal

A secure space where clients track engagement progress, access deliverables, give feedback, and communicate with your team. Replaces email threads and shared Drive folders.

 Automation
Proposal generation

A system that produces a first draft proposal from your templates and the client brief. Your consultant refines and sends. Hours of writing turned into minutes of reviewing.

 Visibility
Engagement dashboard

Real-time visibility across all active client engagements for leadership. Status, milestones, team allocation, and deliverable progress without asking anyone.
Questions

Common questions,
straight answers.

  • What does Opra build for consulting firms?
    Opra builds client portals where clients track engagement progress and access deliverables, proposal generation systems that reduce the time it takes to respond to briefs, onboarding automation for new clients, and internal dashboards that give leadership visibility across all active engagements.
  • Can Opra automate proposal generation for consultants?
    Yes. We build proposal generation systems that use your existing templates and the client brief to produce a first draft automatically. Your consultant reviews, refines, and sends. The time spent per proposal drops from hours to minutes.
  • How does a client portal help a consulting firm?
    A client portal gives your clients visibility into engagement progress without requiring your consultants to write weekly update emails. Deliverables are shared in one place, milestones are tracked, and feedback is collected through the portal. It makes your firm look structured and professional, and reduces the communication overhead on your team.
