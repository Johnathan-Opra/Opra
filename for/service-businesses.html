The problem

Your business is growing.
Operations are
not keeping up.

Service businesses run on client relationships and delivery quality. But as volume increases, the manual processes underneath start to show. Onboarding slows down. Leads get missed. Reporting takes longer. The team spends more time on admin and less on the work clients pay for.

Leads come in and nothing happens automatically. Someone has to notice the inquiry, respond, follow up, and log it. When they're busy, leads wait.

Client onboarding is different every time. No consistent process. Steps get missed. New clients experience a different version of your business depending on who handled them.

Your tools don't connect. Data lives in three places and someone manually moves it between them. CRM, invoicing, calendar. Nothing talks to anything.

No reporting visibility. You don't know your conversion rate, average project value, or which services are growing. The data exists somewhere but no one has pulled it together.

Questions

Common questions,
straight answers.

  • What does Opra build for service businesses?
    Opra builds operational websites that capture and route leads automatically, automation systems that handle client onboarding and follow-up, and dashboards that give you visibility over your operations. Every system is designed to remove manual work and connect the tools your business already uses.
  • What is an operational website for a service business?
    An operational website does more than display your services. It captures leads and routes them to the right place, triggers follow-up sequences, and connects to your CRM or booking system. A client contacts you through the website and the next steps happen automatically.
  • Can Opra automate client onboarding for a service business?
    Yes. When a new client signs or books, the automation handles the sequence: confirmation sent, intake form triggered, welcome information delivered, calendar invite created, and your team notified. No manual steps, no things getting missed.
